I am a fourth-year PhD student in Computer Science at Peking University, where I am very fortunate to be advised by Xiaotie Deng. 
      I obtained my bachelor’s degree in Applied Mathematics from Hua Luogeng Class, Beihang University. 
      My current research interest lies in the intersection between economics and computation. 
      In particular, I focus on players’ strategic behaviors due to information asymmetry, with reflection on the non-realistic assumptions of classic game theory.
